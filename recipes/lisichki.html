Fried potatos with chanterreles and onion

Ingredients

  • 250 g chanterelles;
  • 5-6 sprigs of basil or any other herbs;
  • 2-3 tablespoons of vegetable oil;
  • salt to taste;
  • 700 g potatoes;
  • water - for boiling;
  • 200 ml of milk;
  • 1 tablespoon of butter;
  • pepper - to taste.

STEP-BY-STEP RECIPE

  1. Cut chanterelles into halves or quarters. Chop the greens.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Salt the mushrooms and fry for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, peel the potatoes and cut into large pieces. Boil in salted water for 12-15 minutes. Then drain and return the pot to the stove for another minute or two to evaporate any remaining moisture. Add warm milk, butter and mashed potato with a masher or blender.
  4. Place the mushrooms on top of the mashed potatoes or mix them in. Sprinkle with herbs and pepper.
